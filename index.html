About Us

Junept is motivated by the challenge of delivering quality software which helps reduce the future software maintenance costs to the customers. It is a UK based software development service provider company, specialized in development and licensing of software for Medical Devices, Desktop, Mobile, Web and Cloud environments.

Junept was founded in 2018 and is based in Didcot, near Oxford, England.
